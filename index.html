Open in VLC / MPC‑HC

A tiny helper app that lets you open videos directly in VLC or MPC‑HC from your browser.

Step 1: Install the browser extension

Choose your browser to enable right‑click → "Open in VLC / MPC‑HC".

Firefox Extension Chrome Extension

Step 2: Download & Run the Windows Helper

This small background app opens videos in your chosen player.
